【English pronunciation skills】H Reduction

2021-10-29

连读时后一个单词以'h'的音开头，h被省略/弱读
eg：He -> e His -> is
eg：Does he -> Dose_e
eg：Does he like this kind of food? -> Does_e like this kind of food?
eg：I think he knows. -> I think_e knows.
eg：He has his reasons. -> He has_is reasons.
eg：He just did his job. -> He just did_is job.
